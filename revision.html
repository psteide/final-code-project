Revisions

“Believe in your vision with your own revision"

-P.S. Jagadeesh Kumar

I asked my friend, Alex, to give me some suggestions on the site. He had some major pointers to give.

  • Adjust the colors a bit, try to come up with a better theme
  • Get rid of the bold in every paragraph, it’s too distracting
  • Format the text better, try to get rid of huge blocks of text
  • Try to find oppurtunties to use little graphics

Here is what the page looked like before (click images for larger versions)

Here are the major changes that where made:

In the top section, along with a color theme change, I put the ordered lists in it’s own grid section and gave it some space in between. I also got rid of the numbering as it wasn’t necessary and crowed up the text.

I also took pieces of texts and moved them up into the photos as frame overlays. This allows more information to be accessible, but at the same time not presenting huge blocks of text all at once.

I also use my paragraph tags a bit better to separate out different thoughts better, and got rid of the bold text.
